Creating the future of personalised value based healthcare



Zealth-AI’s mission is to empower patients, their caregivers as well as the clinicians they trust through the most robust digital health intervention platform. Using Zealth in daily routine at hospitals and clinics not only leads to timely interventions but also more patient satisfaction and reduced phone call burdens.
